About Me

I am a PhD student in Astronomy at the University of Padua. I am a associate member of the GALA team (Co-I Prof. Stefano Casotto), which is responsible for the Ganymede Laser Altimeter (GALA) on the JUICE mission. My research focuses on investigating the tidal deformations and rotational dynamics of Ganymede using data from the Ganymede Laser Altimeter (GALA), part of the JUICE mission set to arrive at the Jupiter system in 2031.
