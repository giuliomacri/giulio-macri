About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Padua, under the guidance of Prof. Stefano Casotto and Dr. Hauke Hussmann. My research focuses on investigating the tidal deformations and rotational dynamics of Ganymede using data from the Ganymede Laser Altimeter (GALA), part of the JUICE mission set to arrive at the Jupiter system in 2031.
